FLIPBOOK ANIMATION EVENT

Our first ever animation event, includes all the necessary materials to make a flip book. Please bring a charged phone with flashlight to activate light box. Some extra drawing materials will be available but you are welcome to bring your own supplies to use at the event.

3D RIGGING EVENT

SATURDAY 20TH SEPTEMBER 14:00-17:00

Learn how to create and rig your own armatures in Blender using the provided models or bring your own to try out. You get to learn the basics of weight paint, armatures and try your hand at rigging bipeds and quadrupeds.
This event requires a laptop that can run at least Blender 2.0
