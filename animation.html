Welcome to the animation side of Anisoc, we will be hosting different events relating to parts of animation such as 3D, 2D vector, video editing and a few drawing sessions. No prior experience in animation is needed, come and try your creativity at the events.
All events will be run primarily by our animation rep, Sidnee!

WHAT DO I NEED?

Some knowledge of Blender is preferred for the 3D animation events but not required, some events require a laptop.
Any event requiring specific equipment will be specified!

SEPTEMBER ANIMATION EVENTS

FLIPBOOK ANIMATION EVENT

SATURDAY 13TH SEPTEMBER 13:00-16:00

Our first ever animation event, includes all the necessary materials to make a flip book. Please bring a charged phone with flashlight to activate light box. Some extra drawing materials will be available but you are welcome to bring your own supplies to use at the event.
Event Location: LG.07, 40 George Square

3D RIGGING EVENT

SATURDAY 20TH SEPTEMBER

Learn how to create and rig your own armatures in Blender using the provided models or bring your own to try out. You get to learn the basics of weight paint, armatures and try your hand at rigging bipeds and quadrupeds. This event requires a laptop that can run at least Blender 2.0
Event Location: TBA Stay Tuned!

RICK AND MORTY EVENT

SATURDAY 27TH SEPTEMBER

Learn the difference between vector and 2D animation with a short introduction. You get to try animating with two pre-rigged characters from Rick and Morty and special guest Pickle Rick. A Blender file will be provided to use during this event. This event requires a laptop that can run at least Blender 2.0
Event Location: TBA Stay Tuned!
